# 📚 Preparation & Study Resources

The secret to our growth from **0 to 4 problems** lies in structured preparation. This folder contains the resources we studied before the contest.

### 🎯 Key Focus Areas
- **Algorithmic Patterns:** Cheat sheets for Two Pointers, Sliding Window, and Prefix Sums.
- **Time Complexity:** Notes on Big O notation and optimization.
- **Mock Contests:** Practice sets from previous CALICO and Codeforces rounds.

### 💡 Resources Credits
A significant portion of these materials was curated through the **iCodeGuru** training sessions.
