
# 🌟 UC Berkeley CALICO Informatics: Complete Resource Hub (Fall '26)

This repository is a comprehensive guide to the **CALICO Informatics Competition** hosted by **UC Berkeley**. It includes our team's solutions, problem-solving strategies, and curated materials to help others prepare for future contests.

## 📖 What's Inside?
- **Detailed Solutions:** Step-by-step logic for the 4 problems we solved.
- **Problem Analysis:** Breakdown of all 12 problems from the Fall '26 contest.
- **Preparation Materials:** Helping materials, algorithmic patterns (Two Pointers, Arrays), and resources used for training.
- **The Success Roadmap:** How we improved from solving **0 problems (solo)** to **4 problems (team)** in one year.

## 🏆 Contest Performance
- **Organization:** University of California, Berkeley
- **Team Results:** 4/12 Problems Solved (Alhamdulillah)
